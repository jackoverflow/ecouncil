Why us

Data Secured in Australia

Data stored in, processed in or transiting foreign countries may be subject to their laws and privacy standards. All ecouncil data remains secured in Australia and is subject to Australian privacy protection.

Dedicated Local Support and Services

All support is local to ensure the quality of service required for public organisations. Support is provided during Australian business hours with the option of on-site assistance.

Pricing and Packaging

Is ecouncil forms free?

Yes, all ecouncil forms and functionality are provided at no cost. This includes unlimited users and responders. Councils can upgrade to versions of the tool for enhanced support, integration, workflow and on-site deployment. The ecouncil team can also offer services for any aspect of a local Government process.
